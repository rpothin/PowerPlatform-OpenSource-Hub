# CustomTimelineControl

> The Custom Timeline Control is a PowerApps Component Framework (PCF) control designed to meet customer needs by providing a customisable timeline view for emails in Dynamics 365 in case forms, supporting the display of emails and WhatsApp chats related to parent and ancestor cases.
