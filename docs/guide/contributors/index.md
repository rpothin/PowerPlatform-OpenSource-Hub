# :material-account-group: For Contributors

Open-source contributions are the lifeblood of the Power Platform ecosystem.
Whether you write code, improve documentation, report bugs, or review pull
requests — every contribution helps projects grow and the community thrive.

This section equips you with everything you need to make effective
contributions, from finding your first project to submitting polished pull
requests.

---

## Why Contribute?

!!! success "What you gain"
    - **Skills** — Work on real projects with real users and real code reviews.
    - **Visibility** — Build a public track record that speaks louder than a
      resume.
    - **Network** — Connect with maintainers, other contributors, and the
      broader Power Platform community.
    - **Impact** — Your contribution may be used by thousands of people around
      the world.

## Quick Start

1. **Browse the [Contribute page](../../contribute/index.md)** — Find issues labelled
   `good first issue` or `help wanted` from tracked repositories.
2. **Read a project's CONTRIBUTING.md** — Every project has different
   expectations.
3. **Fork, branch, commit, PR** — Follow the
   [Making Contributions](making-contributions.md) guide.
