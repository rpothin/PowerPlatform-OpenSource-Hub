# :material-account-star: For Users

Open-source tools and libraries power a significant part of the Power Platform
ecosystem. Whether you use a PCF control, a Dataverse SDK, or an automation
framework — you benefit from the work of maintainers and contributors who
share their code freely.

This section helps you **discover the right projects**, **evaluate their
quality**, and **give back** to the community that supports them.

## Why Open Source Matters

!!! info "The Power Platform open-source ecosystem"
    The Power Platform community produces hundreds of open-source projects
    ranging from PCF controls and Dataverse tooling to automation frameworks
    and full application templates. These projects:

    - **Accelerate development** — Use tested building blocks instead of
      starting from scratch.
    - **Share knowledge** — Learn patterns and best practices from real
      production code.
    - **Reduce costs** — Free tools that would otherwise require commercial
      licenses.
    - **Build community** — Connect with other Power Platform professionals
      worldwide.
